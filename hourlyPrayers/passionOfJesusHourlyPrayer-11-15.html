11:15am- Jesus consoles the women
Jesus, only begotten Son of the Father, who will give water to my head, and a fountain of tears to my eyes, that I may, day and night weep and lament my sins? I humbly beseech you by these tears of blood that you shed for me, to grant me true contrition that will soften my hardened heart, cancel my crimes and render me secure in the day of wrath and examination, when you will come to judge the living and the dead, and demand a rigorous account of your blood. Amen.
Fruit- For a contrite heart and sincere repentance.
